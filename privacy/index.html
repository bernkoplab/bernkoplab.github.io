Privacy

For quality assurance and security purposes, and in accordance with Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR, we log the following information:

Date and time of your visit; URL, status, and size of the page you accessed; Your IP address – this may be the temporary one assigned by your internet provider, or that of a proxy, VPN, private relay service, Tor, or another intermediary you use to connect to the internet; (If available) the URL of the referring page; The name, version, and language of your browser

This data is stored locally for 90 days and then automatically deleted. We also use a locally hosted web analytics tool that processes only anonymized data to give us limited insights into how visitors interact with our site. Both the log data and analytics data are used exclusively in-house. We do not combine them with other data, and we do not share them.

The website does not set any cookies.

If the processing of personal data is believed to violate data protection law, or if data protection rights have otherwise been infringed, concerns can be addressed directly to us. We will make every effort to resolve the matter.

In addition, there is the right to lodge a complaint with the Austrian Data Protection Authority: https://www.data-protection-authority.gv.at/
