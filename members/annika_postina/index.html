About

Annika Postina is a pharmacist and PhD candidate focusing on innovative lipid-based drug delivery systems, especially self-emulsifying drug delivery systems (SEDDS) designed to enhance the oral bioavailability of peptide therapeutics by overcoming intestinal enzymatic and mucosal barriers.
