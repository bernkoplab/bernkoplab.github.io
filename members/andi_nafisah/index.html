About

Andi Nafisah is a PhD candidate in Pharmaceutical Sciences at the University of Innsbruck. She develops enzyme-responsive, charge-switchable cubosomes for oral peptide delivery, coupling colloidal stability with intestinal alkaline phosphatase (IAP)–triggered interfacial decomplexation to promote membrane engagement, cellular uptake, and endosomal escape. Her doctoral studies are supported by the Federal Ministry of Education, Science and Research (BMBWF) and administered by OeAD—Austria’s Agency for Education and Internationalisation—through the Ernst Mach Grant. She also serves as a lecturer at Universitas Lampung (UNILA), Indonesia.
