About

Frederic Patka is a PhD candidate at our group and Project Manager at ThioMatrix GmbH. His research focuses on the rational development and mechanistic understanding of lipid-based drug delivery systems, particularly self-emulsifying formulations. At ThioMatrix, he advances the development of novel delivery platforms and coordinates client projects in formulation strategy and translational product development, both with startups and multinational partners.
